A look into my life

Click on *me* to se the website that I made during my freshman year at Pitt.

I am a student currently studying at the University of Pittsburgh as a computer engineering major. I was born on August 31st in Milpitas, California. I lived in Milpitas for the first 12 years of my life where I got the opportunity to experience both a public as well as private form of education, met many new people, and make many new friends. After this time, my parents decided to temporarily relocate half way around the world to my home country India so that I may meet my family there as well as learn more about my cultural background.

The summer of 2005 I left my home in the USA for India. I moved into a house in Hyderabad, India and soon afterwards enrolled into a school called, Oakridge International School. At Oakridge, I met many new students and made a lot of friends as well. During my stay in India, I got a unique glimpse into my culture that I otherwise would not have experienced back in the USA. My friends introduced me to the culture, language, festives, food and many other unique aspects of my culture.
